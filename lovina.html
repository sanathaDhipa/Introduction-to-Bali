About Lovina

A Coastal Serenity

Lovina graces Bali's northern coast with serene beaches and renowned sunrise dolphin-watching tours. A three-hour drive from the airport, it offers uncrowded shores, charming old-Bali vibes, and access to exceptional diving and snorkeling spots. As Bali's top yoga and wellness destination, it provides a tranquil escape with a diverse culinary scene. Explore Lovina for a coastal retreat that captures Bali's serene spirit.

Things to Do in Lovina

Wild Dolphin Tours Lovina

Lovina beckons with its famous wild dolphin encounters along the coast. Join us on a traditional fishing boat for the Wild Dolphin Sunrise Tour, where you can witness the beauty of these creatures in their natural habitat as the day awakens. If you're feeling adventurous, take the opportunity to swim and snorkel with the dolphins in open waters for a truly remarkable experience. Unlike traditional tours, we prioritize a respectful approach, with our experienced captains steering clear of crowds, ensuring a peaceful interaction with 'our' dolphins and creating unforgettable memories.

Sekumpul and Fiji Waterfalls

Nestled in the lush rainforest mountains, Sekumpul and Fiji Waterfalls stand as Bali's most exquisite natural gems, especially in the northern region. Located just an hour east of Lovina, these cascades offer untouched nature, breathtaking vistas, and a tranquil paradise for nature enthusiasts. Revel in the serenity of these falls, away from the crowds, as they remain a hidden sanctuary. For an unforgettable experience and a chance to perch atop a 95-meter-high waterfall, join us on our Lemukih Waterfall Trekking adventure.
